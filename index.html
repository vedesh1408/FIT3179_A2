The Quest for Global Happiness

The Influence of Prosperity on Joy

Our adventure begins by examining the close relationship between a country's economic prosperity, measured by GDP per capita, and the happiness of its citizens. Discover how higher GDP often leads to increased happiness, painting a vivid picture of the importance of economic well-being in the pursuit of contentment.

A World of Happiness Mapped

Continue your exploration by delving into a vivid chloropleth map, showcasing global happiness scores. The map unveils fascinating findings, including the regions where happiness reigns supreme. North and South America, Australia, and Europe are among the bright spots, while the map also sheds light on the challenges faced by Africa and other continents.

As we conclude our journey, we reflect on the enduring pursuit of happiness. It's not just influenced by governments and economic factors; individual choices and the wealth of knowledge available play crucial roles. The quest for happiness transcends borders, and the pursuit of joy is an adventure worth embarking upon.
