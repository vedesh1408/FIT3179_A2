World Happiness Report

Join us on a joyful journey through world happiness! Most of our visualizations will focus on the transformative year of 2016 when the World Happiness Report underwent significant changes.

A World of Happiness Mapped

Start your exploration by delving into a vivid chloropleth map, showcasing global happiness scores. The map unveils fascinating findings, including the regions where happiness reigns supreme. North and South America, Australia, and Europe are among the bright spots, while the map also sheds light on the challenges faced by Africa and other continents.

The Influence of Prosperity on Joy and Life Expectency

Our adventure continues by examining the close relationship between a country's economic prosperity, measured by GDP per capita, and the happiness and life expectency of its citizens. Discover how higher GDP often leads to increased happiness and life expectency, painting a vivid picture of the importance of economic well-being in the pursuit of contentment.

The Fabulous Five vs. The Frowning Five

Explore a unique comparison between the world's top five happiest nations and the bottom five in the happiness ranks. We're pitting measures like freedom, generosity, corruption perceptions, and social support against each other in an extraordinary face-off. Discover what sets the cheeriest countries apart from the least happy ones in this captivating showdown! In 2016, Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway and Switzerland were the happiest and Burundi, Syria, Togo, Afghanistan and Benin were the least happy countries.

Regions Unveiled: Unmasking Happiness Residues

Delve into the fascinating world of regional disparities with the Dystopia Residual Matrix. Uncover the hidden factors that contribute to each region's unique happiness landscape. But what's the Dystopia Residual, you ask? It's a whimsical measure that helps us understand how far each region's happiness level deviates from the hypothetical 'dystopia,' adding an intriguing layer to the story of global well-being.

This whimsical metric measures how far a region's happiness level stands from a make-believe state of ultimate unhappiness, known as 'dystopia.' It's like gauging the gap between a place's real joy and the absolute pits of despair. The bigger the gap, the happier the region is, making it a unique and playful way to understand regional happiness. So, when you see a high Dystopia Residual, it means that region is a long way from the land of gloom, and happiness reigns supreme!
